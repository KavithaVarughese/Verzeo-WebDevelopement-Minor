Mirchi and Mime is a local restaurant located in Powai, Mumbai, in the Indian state of Maharashtra.
Its staff chiefly consists of people with hearing and speech disability.
Most of the activities are undertaken by the staff who communicate with the patrons through sign language, images of whose variations accompany every item in the food menu.
